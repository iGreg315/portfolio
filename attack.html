I really couldn't tell you why, but many of us graphic designers develop a deep and abiding obsession with typography. You'd think we'd get bored with seeing the same shapes and symbols remixed over and over again, but nope. Not even kinda.

My feelings and thoughts on typefaces are so many and so deep that I had to create my own.

This font is essentially the grandchild of a few of my favorite typefaces. I'd say it's mostly Stratum with elements of Avant Garde and Bank Gothic, and I'd like to think that the rest is something original.

I hope to start licensing it once I've completed both a lighter and heavier weight.
